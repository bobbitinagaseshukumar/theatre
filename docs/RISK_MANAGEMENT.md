# Cinema Pro Max — Enterprise Risk, Continuity & DR (Volume 10 · Part 3)

Identify, assess, mitigate, and recover from business, technical, financial, security, AI, and compliance risks.

## Modules
Risk Register · Business Continuity · Disaster Recovery · Operational Resilience · Cyber Risk · Financial Risk · Compliance Risk · Vendor Risk · AI Risk · Incident Response · Crisis Management · Risk Analytics.

## Risk Register Fields
Risk ID · category · owner · business impact · probability · severity · financial exposure · detection date · mitigation status · review schedule · residual risk · trend.

## Risk Categories
Strategic · Operational · Technology · Cloud · Infrastructure · Cybersecurity · Privacy · Financial · Legal · Compliance · AI · Vendor · Third-party · Reputation · Environmental.

## Risk Matrix
| Level | Action |
|---|---|
| Critical | Immediate executive action |
| High | Immediate mitigation |
| Medium | Scheduled mitigation |
| Low | Routine monitoring |
| Informational | Continuous observation |

## Recovery Objectives
| Objective | Target |
|---|---|
| RTO (Recovery Time Objective) | ≤ 15 min |
| RPO (Recovery Point Objective) | ≤ 5 min |
| Max Tolerable Downtime | ≤ 30 min |
| Backup frequency | Continuous (WAL) |
| DR testing | Quarterly |

## Business Continuity Planning
Critical functions, recovery procedures, emergency contacts, alternate/remote operations, communication plans, supply-chain & vendor continuity, customer communication.

## Disaster Recovery
Database/cloud/application/network/infra recovery · backup restoration · cross-region failover · data replication · DNS failover · recovery verification.

## Incident Response Lifecycle
Detection → Classification → Assignment → Containment → Investigation → Recovery → Validation → Documentation → Lessons Learned.

## Crisis Scenarios
System failure · cyber attack · cloud outage · payment gateway failure · data breach · natural disaster · theatre network failure — each with an executive comms + media response plan.
