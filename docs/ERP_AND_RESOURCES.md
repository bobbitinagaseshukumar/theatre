# Cinema Pro Max — ERP & Resource Planning (Volume 10 · Part 2)

Framework for managing financial, human, technical, and infrastructure resources across the program.

## Modules
Financial Management · Budget Planning · Resource Allocation · Procurement · Vendor Management · Contract Management · Asset Management · Cloud Cost Management · Payroll Integration · BI · AI Resource Optimizer.

## Executive KPIs
Total budget · current expenditure · remaining budget · department spend · cloud cost · infra cost · employee cost · vendor payments · revenue forecast · profit projection · cost-optimization score.

## Project Budget Categories
Research · Business Analysis · Architecture · UI/UX · Frontend · Backend · Mobile · Database · AI · Cloud Infra · DevOps · QA · Security · Documentation · Training · Marketing · Operations · **Contingency Reserve**.

## Resource Allocation
Developers · Architects · BAs · UI/UX designers · QA · Security · DevOps · Database · AI · Cloud engineers · PMs · Technical writers · Support.

Workforce planning tracks: availability, skills matrix, assignments, working hours, leave, training, capacity, hiring forecast.

## Procurement & Vendors
- **Procurement:** hardware, software, cloud services, licenses, dev tools, testing devices, security solutions, AI services, third-party APIs.
- **Vendor management:** profiles, contracts, SLAs, invoices, POs, performance ratings, risk assessment, compliance, renewal schedule.
- **Contracts:** number, vendor, start/end, renewal date, payment terms, deliverables, digital signatures, approval workflow.

## Cloud Cost Management
Monitor compute, storage, database, networking, CDN, monitoring, AI compute, backup, traffic, DR costs. Use reserved instances and cost alerts.

## Approval Workflow
Request → Manager Review → Finance Review → Executive Approval → Procurement → Implementation → Audit Logging.

## Financial Forecasting
Monthly/quarterly/annual expenses · cloud growth · hiring needs · revenue growth · profit margin · ROI.

## AI Resource Optimizer (example)
> "Cloud infrastructure spending increased 14% due to AI inference workloads. Recommend reserved compute instances to cut annual operating cost ~18%."

## Audit & Compliance
Financial / procurement / asset / cloud-cost audits · vendor compliance · expense approval logs · budget change history · immutable audit trail.
